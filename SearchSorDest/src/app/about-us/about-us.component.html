About Airline Management

The Airline Management System is a comprehensive online platform where customers can book flights, manage their bookings, and access a wide range of services with ease. Through a web browser, customers can search for flights by destination, date, and airline, add them to their itinerary, and complete the booking process using secure payment methods. Users can log in with their account details, or new customers can quickly create an account by providing their name, contact information, and travel preferences. To access the Airline Management System, customers need only a computer or mobile device with an internet connection. After logging in, they can explore available flights, check their booking status, and make payments using credit cards, debit cards, or online banking options. If a booking has not yet been confirmed, customers may cancel or modify it. The system ensures prompt communication with airlines to manage any changes or cancellations efficiently. In addition to booking flights, the platform offers features such as seat selection, meal preferences, and baggage options, allowing customers to personalize their travel experience. Refunds for canceled bookings will be processed and credited back to the customer's original payment method, whether it's a credit or debit card.



 What We Do ?

The Airline Management System provides a seamless online booking experience for customers through an intuitive web interface. Users can create and sign in to their accounts, search for flights, and complete bookings with ease. The system also enables users to manage their travel itineraries, select seats, and customize their travel preferences. Administrators have access to additional features, allowing them to manage flight details, customer information, and booking confirmations. This ensures that the platform runs smoothly, providing users with the best possible experience.
